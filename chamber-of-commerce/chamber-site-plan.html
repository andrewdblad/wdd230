Site Plan for Whistler, British Columbia Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Whistler Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to:

  1. Act as a regional economic catalyst, promoting business through member services, economic and community development through Whistlers vast outdoor attractions and activities.
  2. Provide a platform for public policy discussions that balance economic prosperity in Whistler.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
